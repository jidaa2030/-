Did You Know?

  • Saudi Arabian citizens make up only about 64% of the total population of the country.
  • Women in Saudi Arabia cannot legally have elective surgery without permission from a male guardian.
  • Around two million Muslims make the hajj (pilgrimage) to Mecca in Saudi Arabia every year.
