Unique and Vibrant Culture

Saudi Arabian culture is deeply rooted in its Islamic heritage. The country is known for its traditional music, dance, and cuisine. Explore the customs and traditions that make Saudi Arabia unique.

Traditional Customs

Saudi Arabia has a rich cultural heritage that includes various customs and traditions:

  • Hospitality: Saudi people are known for their generous hospitality. It is customary to offer guests coffee and dates.
  • Dress: Traditional clothing includes the thobe for men and the abaya for women.
  • Music and Dance: Traditional music includes the use of the oud and the rababah. The ardah is a traditional dance performed on special occasions.

Festivals and Celebrations

Experience the vibrant culture through various festivals and celebrations:

  • Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha: Major Islamic holidays celebrated with prayers, feasts, and family gatherings.
  • Janadriyah Festival: An annual cultural festival that showcases Saudi heritage, including crafts, music, and dance.
  • Riyadh Season: A mega entertainment festival featuring concerts, shows, and cultural events.
