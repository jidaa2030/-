Rich Historical Background

Saudi Arabia has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. The Arabian Peninsula has been a center of trade and culture, connecting the East and West. Learn more about the significant events that shaped the nation.

Timeline of Major Events

  • 570 AD - Birth of Prophet Muhammad
  • 632 AD - Death of Prophet Muhammad and the start of the Rashidun Caliphate
  • 1932 - Unification of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by King Abdulaziz
  • 1973 - Oil embargo and the rise of Saudi Arabia's global influence
  • 2018 - Launch of Vision 2030 aiming to diversify the economy and reduce dependency on oil

Historical Sites to Visit

Explore the rich history of Saudi Arabia by visiting its numerous historical sites:

  • Al-Masmak Fortress in Riyadh
  • Al-Ula's ancient rock formations and tombs
  • Diriyah, the birthplace of the Saudi state
