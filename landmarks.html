Must-Visit Landmarks

Saudi Arabia is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world, such as the Kaaba in Mecca, the Prophet's Mosque in Medina, and the modern marvels of Riyadh. Discover the places you must visit when in Saudi Arabia.

Top Landmarks

  • Kaaba, Mecca: The holiest site in Islam, visited by millions of Muslims during the Hajj pilgrimage.
  • Prophet's Mosque, Medina: One of the largest and oldest mosques in the world, it is the burial place of Prophet Muhammad.
  • Kingdom Tower, Riyadh: A modern architectural marvel with a distinctive sky bridge offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Al-Ula: An ancient city with stunning rock formations and historic tombs, including the famous Hegra (Mada'in Saleh).
  • Edge of the World: A breathtaking cliff formation near Riyadh offering stunning views and a sense of awe.

Natural Wonders

Explore the natural beauty of Saudi Arabia through its diverse landscapes:

  • Red Sea Coast: Known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs, perfect for diving and snorkeling.
  • Asir Mountains: A mountainous region with lush greenery, cool climate, and picturesque villages.
  • Empty Quarter (Rub' al Khali): The largest continuous sand desert in the world, offering a unique desert adventure.
